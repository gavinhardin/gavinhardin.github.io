Predicting Kickstarter Success with Ensemble Classification

  • Tools: Python, Sklearn, Pandas, Gensim, Keras, Matplotlib
  • Concepts: Natural Language Processing, Neural Networks, Discretization, Data Processing

Leading a small group, we developed a heterogeneous ensemble classifier to predict the success of a given Kickstarter project using NLP and trained neural networks. We used Doc2Vec to generate feature vectors for projects' names, which were then used by a logistic regression classifier to predict the success category of a project. Other attributes (fundraising time, goal amount, project category, etc.) were fed into a multilayer perceptron to generate another prediction of success, and then weighted probability averaging was performed to generate a final prediction.
